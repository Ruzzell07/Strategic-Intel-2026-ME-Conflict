To manage the mental workload and avoid misinformation, I utilise **Intelligence Triangulation**:
1. **Collect:** Identify a claim from high-speed sources (e.g., Telegram leaks).
2. **Verify:** Correlate with technical sensors (e.g., NetBlocks outage data or Sentinel-2 imagery).
3. **Contextualise:** Apply academic Cyber Security knowledge to determine the "So What?" for OT Security.
